Department Computer Studies

The program Computer Studies offers three(3) specialty courses; Information Technology, Computer Engineering, and Computer Science. The program focuses technological advancement through education on computer mechanism and programming. It aims to produce highly competitive scientists that prove the quality of its education.

Bachelor Of Science in Computer Engineering

The Bachelor Of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) is a program that embodies the science
and technology of design, development, implementation, maintenance and integration of software and hardware
components in modern computing system and computer-controlled equipment.

The BS Engineering graduates may apply as:

  • Software Developer
  • Test Engineers
  • Computer Programmers
  • Support Specialist
  • Robotics Control Systems Engineers
  • Quality Assurance Manager
  • Systems Analyst
  • Computer Educators
  • TechnoPrenuer
  • Researcher

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) program includes the study of computing concepts and
theories, algorithmic foundations and new developments in computing.
The program prepares students to design and create algorithmically complex software and develop
new and effective algorithms for solving computing problems.

The BS Computer Science graduates may apply as:

  • Software Engineer
  • Systems Software Developer
  • Research and Development Computing Profesional
  • Applicatios Software Developer
  • Computer Programmers
  • System Analyst
  • Data Analyst
  • Quality Assurance Specialist
  • Software Support Specialist

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BS Info Tech) programs includes the study of th utilization
of both hardware and software technologies involving planning, installing, customizing, operating, managing
and administering and maintaining information technology infrastracture that provides computing solutions
to address the needs of an organization.

The BS Infomation Technology Graduates may apply as:

  • Web and Application Developer
  • Junior Databse Administrator
  • Systems Administrator
  • Network Engineer
  • IT Audit Assistant
  • Technical Support Specialist
  • QA Specialist
  • System Analyst
  • Computer Programmer
